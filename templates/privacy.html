Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

1. Introduction

Spendly is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and what rights you have over your data.

2. Information We Collect

We collect only the information necessary to provide the service:

  • Account information: your name, email address, and password (stored as a secure hash).
  • Expense data: amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions you enter manually.
  • Usage data: basic logs such as login timestamps, used solely for security and debugging.

3. How We Use Your Information

Your data is used exclusively to:

  • Provide and display your personal expense records.
  • Authenticate your identity when you sign in.
  • Improve reliability and fix bugs in the application.

We do not use your financial data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond operating the service for you.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with any third parties. Your expense records are private and visible only to you.

5. Data Security

We take reasonable technical measures to protect your data, including password hashing and secure session management. However, no system is completely immune to risk — please use a strong, unique password for your account.

6. Data Retention

Your data is retained for as long as your account is active. When you delete your account, all associated records — including expense history — are permanently removed from our systems.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to access, export, or delete your data at any time. To exercise these rights, use the account settings in your profile or contact us directly.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as the service evolves. We will notify you of significant changes by posting a notice within the app. Your continued use after changes constitutes acceptance.
